About Me

Hey! I’m Rohit Bharadwaj — a curious learner who loves turning ideas into real things through code.

It all started with curiosity — I wanted to understand how technology really works. Over time, I began exploring data, code, and the way information tells stories. I’m still learning, still experimenting, but every new project teaches me something exciting. This website is my space to share that journey — not a finished story, but one that’s being written line by line.

My Skills

  • Python: The language I use to bring ideas to life — from simple logic to complex data analysis.
  • Pandas: My go-to library for cleaning, exploring, and transforming data into something meaningful.
  • NumPy: It helps me handle large sets of numbers efficiently — perfect for math-heavy work and analysis.
  • Matplotlib: Where raw data turns into visuals. I use it to create charts that make data easy to understand.
  • HTML & CSS: Together, they help me design and structure what you see here — making digital ideas look beautiful.
